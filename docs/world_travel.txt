WORLD TRAVEL GUIDE - COMPREHENSIVE EDITION

=== ASIA ===

JAPAN - TOKYO
Tokyo is Japan's capital and largest city.
Must-see: Senso-ji Temple, Shibuya Crossing, Shinjuku, Akihabara, Harajuku.
Best time to visit: March-May (cherry blossoms) or October-November (autumn leaves).
Currency: Japanese Yen (JPY). Language: Japanese.
Visa: Many countries get 90-day visa-free entry.
Food: Sushi, ramen, tempura, yakitori. Try Tsukiji market for fresh seafood.
Transport: JR Pass for bullet trains. Tokyo Metro for city travel.
Budget: Mid-range $100-150/day. Budget $50-70/day.

JAPAN - KYOTO
Former imperial capital with 1600 Buddhist temples and 400 Shinto shrines.
Must-see: Fushimi Inari, Arashiyama Bamboo Grove, Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion), Gion District.
Best time: Spring and Autumn.
Food: Kaiseki multi-course dining, matcha desserts, tofu cuisine.

THAILAND - BANGKOK
Currency: Thai Baht (THB). Language: Thai.
Must-see: Grand Palace, Wat Pho, Chatuchak Market, Khao San Road.
Best time: November to February (cool and dry season).
Visa: 30-day visa on arrival for most countries.
Food: Pad Thai, Tom Yum, Green Curry, Mango Sticky Rice.
Transport: BTS Skytrain, MRT subway, tuk-tuks, river boats.
Budget: Very affordable. $30-50/day budget traveler.

THAILAND - PHUKET
Famous for beaches, nightlife, and island hopping.
Must-see: Phi Phi Islands, Phang Nga Bay, Patong Beach, Big Buddha.
Activities: Snorkeling, diving, elephant sanctuaries.
Best time: November to April.

BALI, INDONESIA
Currency: Indonesian Rupiah (IDR). Language: Balinese/Indonesian.
Must-see: Ubud, Tanah Lot Temple, Tegallalang Rice Terraces, Seminyak Beach.
Best time: April-October (dry season).
Visa: 30-day visa on arrival, extendable.
Food: Nasi Goreng, Satay, Babi Guling, Gado-gado.
Activities: Surfing, yoga retreats, cooking classes, temple visits.
Budget: $40-60/day mid-range.

DUBAI, UAE
Currency: UAE Dirham (AED). Language: Arabic (English widely spoken).
Must-see: Burj Khalifa, Dubai Mall, Palm Jumeirah, Dubai Marina, Desert Safari.
Best time: November to March (cooler weather).
Visa: Many nationalities get visa on arrival or free e-visa.
Food: Shawarma, Hummus, Kebabs, Arabic sweets. Diverse international cuisine.
Transport: Metro, taxis, RTA buses.
Budget: Expensive. $150-300/day.

SINGAPORE
Currency: Singapore Dollar (SGD). Language: English, Mandarin, Malay, Tamil.
Must-see: Gardens by the Bay, Marina Bay Sands, Sentosa Island, Hawker Centers.
Best time: February to April (less rain).
Visa: Visa-free for most nationalities for 30-90 days.
Food: Hainanese Chicken Rice, Laksa, Chilli Crab, Char Kway Teow.
Budget: $80-150/day.

INDIA - GOA
Famous for beaches, Portuguese heritage, and nightlife.
Must-see: Baga Beach, Anjuna Flea Market, Old Goa churches, Dudhsagar Falls.
Best time: November to February.
Currency: Indian Rupee (INR).
Visa: e-Visa available for most countries.

INDIA - MUMBAI
Must-see: Gateway of India, Marine Drive, Elephanta Caves, Dharavi.
Best time: November to February.
Food: Vada Pav, Pav Bhaji, Butter Chicken, Biryani.

MALDIVES
Currency: Maldivian Rufiyaa (MVR). Language: Dhivehi (English widely spoken).
Famous for overwater bungalows, crystal clear water, and coral reefs.
Best time: November to April (dry season).
Visa: 30-day visa on arrival for all nationalities.
Activities: Snorkeling, diving, dolphin watching, surfing.
Budget: Expensive. $300-500/day for resorts.

VIETNAM - HO CHI MINH CITY
Currency: Vietnamese Dong (VND). Language: Vietnamese.
Must-see: Ben Thanh Market, War Remnants Museum, Cu Chi Tunnels, Mekong Delta.
Best time: December to April.
Visa: e-Visa available for most nationalities.
Food: Pho, Banh Mi, Spring Rolls, Bun Bo Hue.
Budget: Very affordable. $25-40/day.

VIETNAM - HANOI AND HA LONG BAY
Must-see: Hoan Kiem Lake, Old Quarter, Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum, Ha Long Bay cruise.
Ha Long Bay has over 1600 limestone islands and islets.
Best time: October to April.

NEPAL - KATHMANDU
Currency: Nepalese Rupee (NPR). Language: Nepali.
Must-see: Pashupatinath Temple, Boudhanath Stupa, Swayambhunath, Everest Base Camp trek.
Best time: October-November or March-May for trekking.
Visa: On arrival for most nationalities.
Activities: Trekking, mountaineering, white water rafting, paragliding.

SRI LANKA
Currency: Sri Lankan Rupee (LKR). Language: Sinhala, Tamil.
Must-see: Sigiriya Rock, Temple of the Tooth, Ella, Mirissa Beach, Yala National Park.
Best time: December to March (west coast), June to September (east coast).
Visa: Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) required.
Food: Rice and curry, Kottu Roti, Hoppers, Coconut Sambol.

=== EUROPE ===

FRANCE - PARIS
Currency: Euro (EUR). Language: French.
Must-see: Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, Notre-Dame Cathedral, Champs-Elysees, Montmartre.
Best time: April-June or September-October.
Visa: Schengen visa required for non-EU citizens.
Food: Croissants, Baguettes, Escargot, Crepes, French wine and cheese.
Transport: Metro, RER trains, buses.
Budget: $150-250/day.

ITALY - ROME
Currency: Euro (EUR). Language: Italian.
Must-see: Colosseum, Vatican City, Trevi Fountain, Pantheon, Roman Forum.
Best time: April-May or September-October.
Visa: Schengen visa for non-EU.
Food: Pizza, Pasta, Gelato, Carbonara, Tiramisu.
Budget: $100-200/day.

ITALY - VENICE
Built on 118 islands connected by canals. No cars allowed.
Must-see: St. Marks Basilica, Grand Canal, Doges Palace, Rialto Bridge.
Activities: Gondola rides, glass blowing in Murano, Burano island.

ITALY - AMALFI COAST
Stunning coastal cliffs, colorful villages, and Mediterranean views.
Must-see: Positano, Ravello, Amalfi town, Path of the Gods hike.

SPAIN - BARCELONA
Currency: Euro (EUR). Language: Spanish, Catalan.
Must-see: Sagrada Familia, Park Guell, Las Ramblas, Gothic Quarter, Camp Nou.
Best time: May-June or September-October.
Food: Paella, Tapas, Churros, Sangria, Jamon.

SPAIN - MADRID
Must-see: Prado Museum, Retiro Park, Royal Palace, Puerta del Sol.
Food: Cocido Madrileno, Bocadillo de Calamares, Churros with chocolate.

UNITED KINGDOM - LONDON
Currency: British Pound (GBP). Language: English.
Must-see: Big Ben, Buckingham Palace, Tower of London, British Museum, Hyde Park.
Best time: June-August or December (Christmas markets).
Visa: Check UK visa requirements for your nationality.
Food: Fish and Chips, Full English Breakfast, Sunday Roast, Afternoon Tea.
Transport: London Underground Tube, buses, black cabs.
Budget: $150-250/day.

NETHERLANDS - AMSTERDAM
Currency: Euro (EUR). Language: Dutch (English widely spoken).
Must-see: Anne Frank House, Rijksmuseum, Van Gogh Museum, Keukenhof Gardens, canal cruises.
Best time: April-May (tulip season) or June-August.
Food: Stroopwafels, Herring, Dutch cheese, Poffertjes mini pancakes.
Transport: Trams, bikes most popular, boats.

GREECE - SANTORINI
Famous for white-washed buildings, blue domed churches, and stunning sunsets.
Must-see: Oia village sunset, Akrotiri archaeological site, Red Beach, Fira town.
Best time: May-October.
Currency: Euro (EUR). Language: Greek.
Activities: Wine tasting, sailing, hiking, beach visits.

GREECE - ATHENS
Must-see: Acropolis, Parthenon, Plaka neighborhood, National Archaeological Museum.
Food: Moussaka, Souvlaki, Gyros, Spanakopita, Baklava.

CZECH REPUBLIC - PRAGUE
Currency: Czech Koruna (CZK). Language: Czech.
Must-see: Prague Castle, Charles Bridge, Old Town Square, Astronomical Clock.
Best time: May-September.
Food: Svickova, Goulash, Trdelnik, Czech beer.
Budget: Affordable. $60-100/day.

PORTUGAL - LISBON
Currency: Euro (EUR). Language: Portuguese.
Must-see: Belem Tower, Jeronimos Monastery, Alfama District, Sintra Palace.
Best time: March-May or September-October.
Food: Pastel de Nata, Bacalhau, Piri Piri Chicken, Port wine.
Budget: Affordable for Europe. $80-120/day.

SWITZERLAND - ZURICH AND INTERLAKEN
Currency: Swiss Franc (CHF). Language: German, French, Italian.
Must-see: Jungfraujoch, Matterhorn, Lake Geneva, Rhine Falls.
Best time: June-September for hiking, December-March for skiing.
Activities: Skiing, hiking, paragliding, scenic train rides.
Budget: Very expensive. $250-400/day.

ICELAND - REYKJAVIK
Currency: Icelandic Krona (ISK). Language: Icelandic (English widely spoken).
Must-see: Northern Lights, Blue Lagoon, Golden Circle, Geysir, Waterfalls.
Best time: June-August (midnight sun) or September-March (Northern Lights).
Visa: Visa-free for many nationalities.
Activities: Whale watching, glacier hiking, hot springs.
Budget: Expensive. $200-300/day.

TURKEY - ISTANBUL
Currency: Turkish Lira (TRY). Language: Turkish.
Must-see: Hagia Sophia, Blue Mosque, Grand Bazaar, Topkapi Palace, Bosphorus cruise.
Best time: April-May or September-October.
Visa: e-Visa available for most nationalities.
Food: Kebabs, Baklava, Turkish Delight, Meze, Turkish tea.
Budget: Very affordable. $50-80/day.

=== AMERICAS ===

USA - NEW YORK CITY
Currency: US Dollar (USD). Language: English.
Must-see: Statue of Liberty, Central Park, Times Square, Brooklyn Bridge, Metropolitan Museum.
Best time: April-June or September-November.
Visa: ESTA for visa waiver countries.
Food: New York Pizza, Bagels, Cheesecake, Hot Dogs, diverse international cuisine.
Transport: Subway, yellow taxis, Uber.
Budget: $150-300/day.

USA - LAS VEGAS
Entertainment capital of the world. Famous for casinos, shows, and nightlife.
Must-see: The Strip, Fremont Street, Grand Canyon day trip, Hoover Dam.
Best time: March-May or September-November (avoid summer heat).

USA - SAN FRANCISCO
Must-see: Golden Gate Bridge, Alcatraz, Fishermans Wharf, Chinatown, Napa Valley.
Best time: September-November.
Food: Sourdough bread, Dungeness crab, Mission burritos, Ghirardelli chocolate.

MEXICO - CANCUN
Currency: Mexican Peso (MXN). Language: Spanish.
Famous for Caribbean beaches, Mayan ruins, and resorts.
Must-see: Chichen Itza, Tulum ruins, Cenotes, Isla Mujeres.
Best time: December to April.
Visa: Visa-free for many nationalities up to 180 days.
Food: Tacos, Guacamole, Ceviche, Enchiladas, Margaritas.
Budget: $50-100/day.

BRAZIL - RIO DE JANEIRO
Currency: Brazilian Real (BRL). Language: Portuguese.
Must-see: Christ the Redeemer, Copacabana Beach, Ipanema, Sugarloaf Mountain, Carnival.
Best time: December to March (summer) or June-September (dry).
Visa: Check requirements for your nationality.
Food: Churrasco BBQ, Feijoada, Acai bowls, Coxinha, Caipirinha.

PERU - MACHU PICCHU AND CUSCO
Currency: Peruvian Sol (PEN). Language: Spanish, Quechua.
Must-see: Machu Picchu, Cusco city, Sacred Valley, Lake Titicaca, Rainbow Mountain.
Best time: May-September (dry season).
Visa: Visa-free for many nationalities.
Activities: Inca Trail trek, visiting ancient ruins, llama spotting.
Food: Ceviche, Lomo Saltado, Guinea Pig, Pisco Sour.

ARGENTINA - BUENOS AIRES
Currency: Argentine Peso (ARS). Language: Spanish.
Must-see: La Boca neighborhood, Recoleta Cemetery, Teatro Colon, Iguazu Falls.
Best time: March-May or September-November.
Food: Asado BBQ, Empanadas, Dulce de Leche, Malbec wine.

CANADA - TORONTO AND VANCOUVER
Currency: Canadian Dollar (CAD). Language: English, French.
Must-see: CN Tower, Niagara Falls, Banff National Park, Stanley Park, Whistler.
Best time: June-August for summer, December-March for skiing.
Visa: Check eTA or visa requirements.
Food: Poutine, Butter Tarts, Nanaimo Bars, smoked salmon.

=== TRAVEL TIPS ===

PACKING ESSENTIALS
- Universal power adapter
- Travel insurance documents
- Photocopies of passport and visa
- First aid kit
- Portable charger and power bank
- Microfiber towel
- Comfortable walking shoes

MONEY TIPS
- Always carry some local cash for small vendors and markets
- Notify your bank before traveling internationally
- Use ATMs at airports or banks for best rates
- Credit cards accepted widely in cities but not rural areas
- Keep emergency cash separate from main wallet

SAFETY TIPS
- Register with your country's embassy when visiting high-risk areas
- Keep copies of important documents in cloud storage
- Use hotel safe for passports and valuables
- Be aware of common tourist scams in each destination
- Get travel insurance that covers medical evacuation

HEALTH TIPS
- Check required vaccinations for each destination
- Carry prescribed medications with doctor's letter
- Drink bottled water in developing countries
- Use sunscreen and mosquito repellent in tropical areas
- Have travel health insurance with good medical coverage

BUDGET TRAVEL TIPS
- Book flights 6-8 weeks in advance for best prices
- Travel during shoulder season for lower costs
- Use hostels or Airbnb instead of hotels
- Eat at local markets and street food stalls
- Use public transport instead of taxis
- Get city tourist cards for museums and transport discounts
